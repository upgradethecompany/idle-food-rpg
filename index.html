Welcome to Idle Label Puzzle (v1.0). Here are some rules:
  • Your goal is to Upgrade the labels from the positive bars and downgrade the labels from the negative bars.
  • Labels are randomized after each upgrade/downgrade.
  • The upgrades remain on the bar it was at the time of the upgrade, the randomized labels apply to the new upgrade/downgrade to be done.
  • Click or Hover the bars to bars to speed up or slow down.
  • Labels like "G1A1" or "G1_interA" affects not only the label but the whole tab.
  • Prestige/Reset keeps your Prestige Points so if you get stuck in negative values your only choice is to reset.
